Our Story

Where Magic Meets
Technology

In 1993, Foxy's Entertainment changed birthday parties forever. We opened the doors of Foxy's Palace with a single mission: create an experience that no child — or adult — would ever forget. Our secret? The world's first truly intelligent animatronic performers.


Unlike anything else on the market, our animatronics can walk freely through the restaurant, hold conversations, play games, open doors, sing, dance, and genuinely interact with every single guest. Powered by proprietary A.I. developed exclusively by Foxy's Entertainment, they aren't just machines — they're family.

Meet the Gang!

Powered by cutting-edge A.I., our four incredible animatronics are the heart and soul of Foxy's Palace. They're not performers — they're your new best friends.

The face of Foxy's Palace! This energetic blue-and-purple fox is always at the center of the action — cracking jokes, leading songs, and making every single guest feel like the star of the show. Got adults at the party? Foxy switches gears and keeps the grown-ups entertained too!

This fierce green bunny doesn't sit still for a second! Hide-and-seek, tag, football, basketball — Hopps organises every game and makes sure no child gets left out. She and Foxy are an unstoppable duo when it comes to keeping the kids absolutely buzzing with excitement.

Draccon thinks he's the coolest dragon in the universe — and honestly? He might be right. This fire-breathing showoff pulls off the most jaw-dropping stunts and shreds on his electric guitar leaving kids completely stunned. Penny's always nearby to make sure he doesn't go too far.
